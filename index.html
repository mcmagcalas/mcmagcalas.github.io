Poverty in the Philippines

Poverty is the state of not being able to afford basic necessities like clothes, food, and education. It has been a serious problem in the Philippines with over a quarter of its total population experiencing it even as the country makes big strides of development. Children of families that suffer from poverty tend to not get enough education, or no education at all.
